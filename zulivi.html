01 / Threshold

The Boundary
I Stepped Into

I stepped into the gap between traditional medical traditions and modern trust systems. Traditional medicine carries centuries of knowledge, yet its supply chain is fragmented, and vulnerable to quality issues — especially in emerging markets like Vietnam.

02 / Approach

Navigating
Between Worlds

I approached the problem as both a founder and a learner: listening to herbalists, understanding regulatory systems, and translating cultural practices into scalable, verifiable processes. Rather than replacing tradition, I treated technology as a supporting infrastructure — a way to protect authenticity, not erase it.

Modernized Traditional Products

Blending age-old herbal knowledge with contemporary quality assurance to build trust in traditional medicine.I tried to apply modern approach and technology, such as blockchain for tracebility, QR code for product information, to traditional products.

Customer-centric

Focusing on educating and engaging customers to appreciate the value of traditional medicine through transparent sourcing and quality assurance.

03 / Artifact

Built at the
Intersection

Redesign Packaging

I redesigned ZULIVI's packaging to reflect the blend of tradition and modernity. Using natural textures and minimalist design, also, there's the expiration date for ensuring the best quality and blockchain stamp which allow the orgin tracebility. The packaging communicates trustworthiness while honoring the herbalist heritage.

Digitalize the Process

I digitalized the process of traditional medicine supply, from sourcing the delivery to the end customers. Customers can easily purchase herb on online platform, with many promotion programs. By integrating blockchain technology, we ensure transparency and traceability, allowing customers to verify the authenticity and quality of their herbal products.

04 / Reflections

What Crossing
Taught Me

I learned that innovation succeeds only when it respects cultural roots. Trust is not created by algorithms alone — it emerges when systems align with lived practices and local realities.
How technology can help to solve the challenges of traditional medicine without erasing its heritage.

I see ZULIVI evolving into a cross-border model for ethical, transparent traditional medicine — proving that heritage and modern systems can grow together. But moreover, ZULIVI is my passion for building a bridge between the old and the new, form a community that values maintaining body and health.
