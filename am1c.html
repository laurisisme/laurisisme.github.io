02 / Approach

Navigating
Between Worlds

Through interviews and field recording, we treated sound as cultural evidence — a bridge between memory and place.

Collected personal narratives from diverse city residents, then wove them into speculative soundscapes that imagine future urban experiences.

Sound Centric

03 / Artifact

Built at the
Intersection

Sonic Narratives

We used Adobe Audition to produce sonic narratives across four Chinese cities: Shanghai, Qingdao, Rizhao, Hangzhou. Each narrative blends field recordings with personal stories to create immersive soundscapes that reflect the unique cultural identities of these urban environments.

Social Media Reels

Created engaging social media reels to share the sonic narratives, utilizing dynamic visuals and sound design to captivate audiences and promote cultural appreciation. Our Instagram platform reaching 5,000+ views.

04 / Reflections

What Crossing
Taught Me

Sound reveals what visuals often miss: rhythm, emotion, intimacy.
By blending personal stories with ambient soundscapes, we can craft richer, more empathetic urban narratives that resonate on a human level. It also shows that some sounds can envoke powerful memories and emotions tied to place.
05 / The Unknown

The Horizon

I want to explore sound as a tool for cultural preservation and empathy-building. I believe sound is a powerful medium to connect people to place and to each other, especially in our increasingly digital and visual world.
