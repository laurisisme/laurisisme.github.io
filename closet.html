01 / Threshold

The Boundary
I Stepped Into

I stepped into the everyday tension between personal choice and cognitive overload. People own clothes — but often feel they have “nothing to wear.” This is a human problem disguised as a fashion problem. And the core for it is people don't have information about what they own.

.
02 / Approach

Navigating
Between Worlds

I started from behavior, not technology: observing how people choose outfits, forget what they own, and overconsume. Virtual Closet is an all-in-one digital wardrobe that visualizes clothing, recommends outfits, and encourages sustainable habits.

Digital Wardrobe Management with Gamified Closet Constraints

Users catalog their clothing using images, organized by type, color, and usage frequency, providing personalized insights into wardrobe composition. Each new item added requires conscious allocation of space, reinforcing awareness of consumption.

AI-Assisted Outfit Suggestions

The application provides outfit recommendations based on the user’s existing wardrobe, usage history, and personal preferences

03 / Artifact

Built at the
Intersection

Behavioral Approach of Business Design

I conducted market research and user interviews to identify pain points in wardrobe management and sustainable fashion. I apply nudge theory to design features that subtly guide users towards mindful consumption and outfit choices by charging in the slot added to their game.

AI Application on Game Design

Built using React Native for cross-platform compatibility, the app integrates TensorFlow.js for on-device image recognition, enabling users to catalog clothing items through photos. A recommendation engine suggests outfits based on user preferences and wardrobe data.

04 / Reflections

What Crossing
Taught Me

Sustainability is not a moral lecture — it’s a design challenge. When decision-making becomes easier, people naturally make better choices.
True innovation happens at the intersection of disciplines. By blending behavioral science, AI, and game design, I created a solution that is not only functional but also engaging and impactful.
