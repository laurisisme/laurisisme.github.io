01 / Threshold

The Boundary
I Stepped Into

I stepped into the fragile space between independence and vulnerability. For many elderly people, a fall is not just an accident — it’s a turning point. But many of these accident aren't not detected in time due to the lack of immediate assistance. And the demand for it is increasing with aging population worldwide.

02 / Approach

Navigating
Between Worlds

ElderCare is An elderly health monitoring system for real-time risk detection and timely care. We combined technology for better health checking, especially fall detection, with human-centered design to respect user dignity and autonomy.

Real-Time Fall Detection via Camera Tracking

This technology leverages pose recognition from live camera footage to identify falls or risky movements. Importantly, it does this without the need for wearables, making it a seamless addition to any home.

Instant Alerts to Caregivers

ElderCare include instant alerts to caregivers, which ensures a rapid response to any detected health risk. This immediate notification can significantly improve the chances of quick intervention and care.

03 / Artifact

Built at the
Intersection

AI-powered fall-detection system

In two days, our team built an AI-powered fall-detection system using TensorFlow and Google Teachable Machine, trained on 350 datasets. We also worked with Copilot to intergrate the front-end and back-end functionalities.

Business Development

Beyond the model, I developed market research and a business plan, pitching the project at SGA Demo Day.
